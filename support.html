Inside the App

Fastest places to start

  • Billing and subscription access: open the Billing screen in Settings.
  • Account deletion: open Settings and use Delete Account.
  • Legal and privacy details: open the links in Settings.
Support Topics

What ChurchOps support can help with

  • Sign-in problems or account access issues.
  • Invite, team membership, admin, or owner-transfer questions.
  • Billing, subscription access, seat limits, or restore-purchase questions.
  • Account deletion or last-owner team deletion questions.
  • Google Drive, workflow, or external integration issues.
  • App errors, unexpected behavior, or launch problems on iPhone or Mac.
Helpful Details

Include these details when you contact support

  • The email address on the ChurchOps account.
  • The team name.
  • The device and platform you are using.
  • Any error message you saw.
  • The approximate date and time the issue happened.
